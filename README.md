# Dexter

**Dexter** is a psychological crime thriller TV series about a forensic expert who leads a double life as a **vigilante serial killer**.

## Main Characters
- **Dexter Morgan** – Blood spatter analyst by day, killer by night  
- **Debra Morgan** – Dexter's adoptive sister and police officer  
- **Harry Morgan** – Dexter’s adoptive father, taught him the "Code"  
- **Rita Bennett** – Dexter’s girlfriend and later wife

## Key Themes
> "Monsters don’t get to live happily ever after."  
> — Dexter Morgan

- Justice vs. morality  
- Dual identity  
- Psychological trauma  
- Vigilantism
